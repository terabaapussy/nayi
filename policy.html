Our Policies

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment details (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Order history and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, improve our services, and communicate with you about your orders and promotions.

Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Returns

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items should be in their original packaging.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you of the refund status. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Shipping Policy

We offer free standard shipping on all orders.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days.

Delivery Time

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days after processing. Delivery times may vary depending on your location.
